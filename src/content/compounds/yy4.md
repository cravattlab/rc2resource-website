YY4 is an alpha-chloroacetamide that covalently and irreversibly modifies cysteine-54 (C54) of the MPC2 subunit of the mitochondrial pyruvate carrier (MPC). YY4 has been shown to engage MPC2 in human T-cells with an IC50 of 0.88 Î¼M (2h treatment).
