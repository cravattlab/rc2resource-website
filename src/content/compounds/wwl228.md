WWL228 is a structurally related control compound for <a href="#wwl229" class="js-scroll-trigger">WWL229</a>. It does not inhibit Ces3 or Ces1f recombinantly expressed in HEK293T cells (IC50 &gt; 20 Î¼M). WWL228 does not promote differentiation of preadipocytes or inhibit basal lipolysis in mature adipocytes.
